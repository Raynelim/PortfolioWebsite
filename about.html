How I Think

My Design Philosophy.

01

Clarity over cleverness

The best design is the one people don't notice. If someone has to think about how to use something, it's already failed. I design for the half-second it takes someone to figure out where to tap.

02

Build to understand

I don't stop at the frame. Building what I design forces me to make decisions that Figma doesn't ask for — and those decisions always make the product better.

03

Details are the design

Spacing, weight, timing, colour — these aren't finishing touches. They're the work. I obsess over the details because that's where the difference between good and great lives.

04

Design for real people

I don't design for portfolios or awards. I design for the person who's tired, distracted, and just trying to get something done. Empathy is the most important tool I have.
